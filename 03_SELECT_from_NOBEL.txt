12. Apostrophe

> Find all details of the prize won by EUGENE O'NEILL

Escaping single quotes. You can't put a single quote in a quote string directly.
You can use two single quotes within a quoted string.

SELECT * FROM nobel
WHERE winner = "EUGENE O'NEILL";

Answer:

yr    subject      winner
-------------------------------------
1936  literature   Eugene O'Neill
